DLB Integrators Website Plan

Site Name

DLB Integrators - This name represents a business that specializes in designing, building, and integrating custom automated systems for industrial and food production environments. It is professional and clearly indicates the scope of services offered.

Site Purpose

The website will serve as a digital brochure and information hub for DLB Integrators. It will showcase our experience in designing and installing stainless steel, washdown-rated automation systems primarily for the food processing industry. The site will explain our services, feature images of our equipment, and provide contact and inquiry forms for prospective clients.

Scenarios

  • “I want to do more automation in our facility — how can DLB help me?”
  • “I have some automated equipment already but I need to connect them and add conveyors. Can DLB help me?”

Color Schema

Primary Color: Dark Blue
Used for: header, footer, headings, buttons.

Accent Color: Golden Orange
Used for: highlights, button hover, links, icons.

Typography

Font: Segoe UI
Used for: all text including headings and body. This system font provides a clean, modern, professional appearance and ensures good readability across devices.
